About UnPlastic: Restoring Realism to AI Art

UnPlastic is a free, high-performance web tool specifically designed to fix the common flaws of AI-generated images. Whether you use Z-Image-Turbo, Qwen-Image, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or DALL-E, AI models often produce portraits and landscapes with an overly smooth, blurry texture. UnPlastic peels away this synthetic, "plastic look," restoring a natural, photographic feel to your generations.

The Problem with AI Image Textures

While generative AI is incredible at composition, it frequently struggles with micro-details. Skin looks like plastic, fabrics lack weaves, and shadows become flat gradients. Traditional photo editors try to fix this with basic "Unsharp Mask" or "Clarity" filters, which usually destroy the image by creating ugly white halos around edges and exaggerating digital artifacts.

How UnPlastic Fixes It

UnPlastic uses a custom-built processing engine tailored for AI art, focusing on smart detail recovery:

  • Intelligent Micro-Texture Recovery: Our algorithms separate image frequencies to safely extract and enhance micro-details (like skin pores, dirt, and fabric) while strictly protecting high-contrast edges from haloing.
  • Organic Adaptive Grain (Grit): Static noise overlays look cheap. UnPlastic generates dynamic, luminance-responsive digital grain that mimics real camera sensors. It naturally blends into shadows to break up artificial AI gradients.
  • Depth & Structure Restoration: Eliminate the flat "AI haze" and restore natural 3D volume to faces and environments with deep mid-tone contrast adjustments.

100% Private, Local & Secure

Your privacy is guaranteed. UnPlastic is a completely client-side application running on the HTML5 Canvas API and WebAssembly. Your images never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server. Enjoy lightning-fast, desktop-level post-processing directly in your browser.
